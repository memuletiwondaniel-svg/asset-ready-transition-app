

## Fix: Erratic Follow-up Pills + Human-Friendly "Read" Action Labels

### Problem 1: Erratic / Nonsensical Suggested Action Pills

The 3-tier regex extraction (Tier 2 and Tier 3 in `ORSHChatDialog.tsx` lines 1029-1069) aggressively matches any bullet list under headers containing words like "options", "steps", "next", "would you like". This captures instructional text, error recovery steps ("Contact the document controller"), and metadata lines as action pills тАФ producing nonsensical suggestions like "Document Number: 6523-EXTR-C008...", "Supplier: EXTR (Exterran)?", "Status: IFA?", "Revision: 01A?".

**Fix** (`ORSHChatDialog.tsx`):
- Add a pill sanity filter after extraction: reject items that match metadata patterns (start with "Document Number:", "Status:", "Revision:", "Supplier:", "Title:") or contain full document numbers (match `ASSAI_DOC_NUMBER_REGEX`)
- Reject pills longer than 80 characters (these are usually prose, not actions)
- Reject pills that are purely informational (start with "Contact", "You could", "The document")
- Cap at max 5 pills to prevent overflow

### Problem 2: "Read and summarise" sends raw doc numbers

When the user clicks a "Read & Analyse" button or a follow-up pill, the message sent is `"Read and summarise PROCESS BASIS OF DESIGN*** DOCUMENT NUMBER CHANGED FROM 2ND REVISION 6523-EXTR-C008-ISGP-G00000-PX-7770-00001-000_02A***"` тАФ using the raw title field which may contain DMS notes and long document numbers. Users cannot parse these.

**Fix** (two files):

**`StructuredResponse.tsx`** тАФ Change the `onRead` call text:
- `DocumentQuickActions` (line 23): Change from `Read and summarise ${doc.title}` to `Read and interpret ${toTitleCase(doc.title.split('***')[0].trim())}` тАФ strip DMS annotations after `***`, use title case
- `DocActionButtons` (line 59): Same change тАФ use clean title, never raw doc number
- Tooltip text (line 65): Change "Read & summarise" тЖТ "Read & interpret"

**`supabase/functions/ai-chat/index.ts`** тАФ Change followup pill text generated by the backend:
- All instances of `Read and summarise the most relevant ${subjectLabel} document` тЖТ `Read and interpret the most relevant ${subjectLabel} document`
- `Read & summarise the first document` тЖТ `Read and interpret the first document`
